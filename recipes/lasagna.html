Cheesy beef and spinach lasagne

Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 to 4 minutes or until softened. Increase heat to medium-high. Add mince. Cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up mince, for 5 minutes or until browned.

Add carrot and tomato paste to mince mixture. Stir to combine. Add tomato, oregano and 1/4 cup cold water. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 30 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Season with salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 medium brown onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 600g lean beef mince
