Cucumber, mint & feta salad

Nothing adds a refreshing crunch to a meal quite like cucumber. The versatile veg is the star in yoghurt-based sauces like tztaztiki and raita, which compliments grilled meats and curries. They also make a great addition to salads, adding a freshness like no other.

Ingredients

  • 1 small red onion, halved, thinly sliced
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) water
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
