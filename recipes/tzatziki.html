Christina and her Yia Yia Rose share this simple recipe for authentic Greek tzatziki. Serve with pita bread or with other dishes.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Greek yoghurt
  • 2 cucumbers, skin removed
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  1. Step 1

    Strain the Greek yoghurt in a Chux wipe (or any other material that strains) for 30 minutes in the fridge.

  2. Step 2

    Grate the cucumbers and strain (as above).

  3. Step 3

    Combine the garlic, oil, herbs and salt in a large bowl with yoghurt and cucumber and mix. Cover and refrigerate.

  4. Step 4

    Serve chilled with pita bread for dipping or with dishes.
