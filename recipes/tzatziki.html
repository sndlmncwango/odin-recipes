Christina and her Yia Yia Rose share this simple recipe for authentic Greek tzatziki. Serve with pita bread or with other dishes.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Greek yoghurt
  • 2 cucumbers, skin removed
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

    STEPS

  1. Strain the Greek yoghurt in a Chux wipe (or any other material that strains) for 30 minutes in the fridge.

  2. Grate the cucumbers and strain (as above).

  3. Combine the garlic, oil, herbs and salt in a large bowl with yoghurt and cucumber and mix. Cover and refrigerate.

  4. Serve chilled with pita bread for dipping or with dishes.
